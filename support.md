## Frequently Asked Questions

### How does the app work?
Poker Situations presents you with real tournament poker scenarios. You choose an action and receive instant feedback explaining the optimal play.

### Does the app require an internet connection?
An internet connection may be required to download new situations. Once loaded, situations can be used offline.

### Do I need to create an account?
No. Poker Situations does not require account creation.

### Is my data saved online?
No. All progress is stored locally on your device.
